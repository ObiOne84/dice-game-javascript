ROLL DICES GAME!

About the game

The roll dice game is fun entertaintment for everyone involving chance and luck!

  • The player can roll two dices simultaneously.
  • The score is calculated by adding the total value of the number displayed on the dice.
  • Every time you roll double, the score is multiplied by two.
  • The highest score wins.
  • Each dice roll equal one battle.
  • To win the game, you must win 11 battles.
